- **Asset-heavy**: Owns inventory (unlike pure classifieds like OLX)

## 3. CRM AS A CHANNEL — DEEP DIVE

### What "CRM Channel" Means at Cars24-Scale Retail
CRM (Customer Relationship Management) as a channel refers to **all owned, direct communication touchpoints** used to engage existing leads, prospects, and past customers through their lifecycle. Unlike paid acquisition, CRM focuses on **nurturing, re-engagement, and retention** using first-party data.

| Sub-Channel | Use Cases | Key Metrics |
|---|---|---|
| **Email** | Nurture drips, price alerts, booking reminders, post-purchase | Open Rate, CTR, Unsubscribe Rate, Conversion Rate |
| **SMS** | OTP, booking confirmations, price drop alerts, follow-ups | Delivery Rate, Click Rate, Opt-out Rate |
| **WhatsApp (Business API)** | Discovery bot, test drive booking, lead qualification, post-sale | Read Rate (98% open), Reply Rate, Bot Containment Rate, CSAT |
| **Push Notifications** | App re-engagement, price alerts, inventory updates | Delivery Rate, Open Rate, CTR |

## 5. CARS24 RETAIL BUYING FUNNEL — CRM TOUCHPOINTS

### Buy-Side (Consumer Purchasing a Car)

```
Session → Browse/Search → Shortlist → Web Inquiry/Lead
    ↓
Lead Capture (Form, WhatsApp, Call)
    ↓
Lead Qualification (CRM scoring, AI qualification)
    ↓
Test Drive Booking (WhatsApp bot, call, app)
    ↓
Test Drive Visit (~60 min at hub)
    ↓
Financing/Loan Application (LOANS24 instant approval)
    ↓
Negotiation / Price Acceptance
    ↓
Booking / Payment
    ↓
Documentation & RC Transfer
    ↓
Delivery (home delivery available)
    ↓
Post-Purchase (warranty, service, referral)
```

**CRM Interventions at Each Stage**:
- **Post-Browse, No Action**: Price drop alerts, similar car suggestions (push/email/WhatsApp)
- **Lead Captured, No Booking**: Nurture drip (3-7 touches), WhatsApp discovery bot, AI outbound call
- **Booking Made, No Show**: Reminder SMS/WhatsApp, appointment confirmation call
- **Test Drive Done, No Purchase**: Follow-up call with offers, financing options, alternative inventory
- **Purchase Done**: Delivery updates, RC transfer status, warranty info, NPS survey
- **Post-Delivery**: Service reminders (FourDoor), insurance renewal, referral program, re-purchase nudge

### Sell-Side (Individual Selling a Car)

```
Session → Online Evaluation (AI-powered web quote)
    ↓
Appointment Booking (branch or home inspection)
    ↓
Inspection (~200+ checkpoints)
    ↓
Auction (live bidding by 10,000+ channel partners)
    ↓
Price Offer to Seller
    ↓
Acceptance / Negotiation / Follow-up
    ↓
Documentation & Payment (often same day)
    ↓
RC Transfer (Seller Kavach for zero-liability)
```

**Key Funnel Insight**: ~90% of users who receive a web quote drop off before visiting the store for inspection. ~1/3 of completed transactions happen via follow-up (not first-attempt conversion).

## 6. KEY METRICS FOR CRM CHANNEL PERFORMANCE

### Funnel Metrics (Stage-by-Stage)
| Metric | Formula | Benchmark Context |
|---|---|---|
| **Lead Volume** | Total leads captured (form + call + WhatsApp + walk-in) | Segment by source for channel ROI |
| **Lead-to-Contact Rate** | Contactable leads / Total leads | Filters fake emails/numbers |
| **Contact-to-Booking Rate** | Bookings / Contactable leads | CRM nurture effectiveness |
| **Booking-to-Show Rate** | Actual visits / Bookings | Reminder & confirmation effectiveness |
| **Show-to-Conversion Rate** | Purchases / Visits | Sales + financing effectiveness |
| **Overall CVR** | Purchases / Total leads | End-to-end funnel health |
| **Speed-to-Lead** | Time from lead capture to first contact | <5 min = 21x higher qualification rate |
| **Follow-up Conversion Rate** | Sales from follow-up / Total follow-ups | ~1/3 of Cars24 sales come from follow-up |
