## Channel summary

CRM at Cars24 operates as a full-funnel retargeting and engagement engine across Buy, Sell, VAS, Insurance, and Lending verticals. The team of 4 (2 Deputy Managers, 1 Team Lead, 1 Associate Program Manager) manages 25.8Cr+ annual communications at ₹3.1Cr spend, driving BIs, BCs, Visits, and Deliveries through WhatsApp, SMS, Push, Email, IVR, and RCS — with WhatsApp dominating at ~99% of buy-side sends.
